About Me

My name is Anita. Let me tell you a bit about myself.

I am a UX/UI and graphic designer based in Toronto, who loves to work with a creative mind. The focus of my work surrounds the concepts of digital solutions that enhance user experiences through an artistic and logical flow. During my personal time, I love working on digital design projects, spending time with my pups, traveling, and creating digital content for my Instagram food account!

Beyond digital designs, I also love to explore the depths of designs around the world through the lens of architecture, interior design, and cinematography. Every step has been an increment to broaden my perspectives and insights in the world of design.
